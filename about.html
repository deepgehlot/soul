The journey of Soul Brewing Company began with the passion and dedication of its brewer and owner, whose brewing career took off at VLB Berlin, one of the world's most prestigious brewing schools. As he wandered the storied back alleys of Berlin, the city where beer is more than just a beverage, it's a way of life, the seeds of his dream took root. After honing his skills by brewing for various breweries across the country, the desire to establish his own brewery and taproom only grew stronger. In response to the tumultuous global events of 2020, he saw an opportunity for a renaissance in traditional beer styles in Jaipur.

We are the first craft beer brand in South India to receive a factory outlet licence to offer takeaway of fresh craft beer in 2L soul Growlers from our flagship beer garden – soul Brewing Co. OMR in Bangalore.

We are the first craft beer brand in South India to supply kegs of fresh craft beer to restaurants, bars, pubs, hotels & events across Bangalore & Hyderabad.

We are the first craft beer brand in South India to manufacture, package and distribute our fresh craft beer in 500 ML soul Crowlers that are available for sale at retail partner locations across Bangalore.

It was with immense anticipation and excitement that brothers Harpal and Tarun launched Soul Brewing Company in 2022. Located on M.I. Road in Jaipur, this avant-garde distribution brewery boasts a taproom that not only serves authentic craft beer styles but also tantalizes taste buds with soulful food offerings. Tarun, having trained rigorously at Le Cordon Bleu in London, demonstrates that he knows exactly what he's doing as a skilled chef. As soon as you step inside the uplifting atmosphere of Soul's taproom, you're instantly transported away from the burdens of the outside world. With meticulously curated music setting the perfect ambiance against a background of expertly crafted beers and globally inspired cuisine, patrons can sip and savor everything from modern cocktails to timeless brews. The Soul Brewing Company genuinely offers an unparalleled experience that begs the question – what more could one ask for?

Yeast has a huge influence on taste. Understanding and managing yeast is key to producing great beers. Our yeast lab ensures that we keep all our house strains happy and smiling. Happy yeasts make great beer

We’re deeply committed to ensuring our beer remains fresh right until you take your first sip. Our beer is made, stored, transported and served at the optimum temperature via an end-to-end cold chain, so that you get the best craft beer experience, every single time. For us, Cold is Gold!

We are a (near) Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) brewery. 100% of the rainwater is harvested, effluents from the brewery are processed in an STP and converted into water that is used for cleaning dishes in the kitchen, providing water for our cooling tower & flushing our toilets.

All our malts come from international artisanal maltsters who are as passionate about their products as we are about our beer. They use heritage malting techniques and modern technology to provide us with the best, most consistent malts.
